Tips and tricks for journaling

1.Find a time that works best for you.

Whenever you are free start to journal. You can write in the morning allowing you to plan out your day, relflect on how you can deal with anticipated of the day, and or what you excited for throught the day. Try writing at different times of the day and see how you feel.



2.Find qutoes that inspire you.

When you're feeling stuck look online for qutoes that inpsire you. How does the qutoe make you feel? How can you apply the qutoe to your life?
